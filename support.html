Before Reporting

  • Include your iPhone model and iOS version.
  • Tell us which app language you are using.
  • For speech issues, mention the spoken language and whether Speech Recognition permission is allowed.
  • For weather issues, mention whether Location permission is allowed.
  • Please do not send private diary text unless you intentionally choose to share it.

Known Launch Notes

  • Speech recognition availability depends on Apple's system support for your device, region, and language.
  • Weather is optional and requires Location permission plus network access to Open-Meteo.
  • Premium skins are optional visual content. The diary flow remains usable without purchasing them.
