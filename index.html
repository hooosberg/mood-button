Privacy

Your journal is not a public product feed

Qwen3 diary generation is designed to run on device through Apple MLX. Diary text, recordings, search terms, and skin preferences stay in the app sandbox on your device. Speech recognition uses Apple Speech. Optional weather uses Open-Meteo with rounded coordinates after Location permission.
